Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

This site is a small multiplayer chess game. We collect as little data as possible — there are no accounts, no tracking pixels and no analytics scripts.

What we process

  • Display name. The name you type when creating or joining a game is sent to the server and shown to the other players in your room. It is held in the server's memory only for the lifetime of the game room and is deleted when the room expires (about 15 minutes after the last player leaves). Use any name you like — it does not have to identify you.
  • Game data. Moves, drops, clock times and piece requests are relayed through the server to the other players in your room. They are kept in memory only and are not logged or stored after the room expires.
  • Connection data. Like any web server, our hosting provider (Render) may process your IP address and standard request metadata to deliver the site and may keep short-lived technical logs. See Render's privacy policy.

What is stored in your browser

  • Reconnection token. While you are in an online game, a random token is kept in your browser's sessionStorage so you can rejoin your seat if your connection drops. It is deleted when you close the tab.
  • No cookies. The game itself sets no cookies.

Advertising

If advertising (such as Google AdSense) is enabled on this site in the future, ad providers may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads, and visitors in regions that require it will be shown a consent dialog before any advertising cookies are set. This policy will be updated before any such change takes effect.

Children

The site does not knowingly collect personal information from children; no personal information is required to play.

Your choices

Because nothing is stored about you after a game room expires, there is usually nothing to delete. If you have any privacy question or request, contact the site operator at [your-contact-email].
